Scope of Engagement

This letter confirms the terms of engagement for the Peak Season Readiness & Stock Control App programme (the "Programme") as agreed between Decoded Ops and TackleBag Ltd.

The Programme comprises two workstreams:

  • Workstream 1 — Peak Season Operations Consultancy (£4,500): 6 on-site days at TackleBag's Twickenham premises (4 visits in Weeks 1–4, 2 visits in Weeks 5–8), covering warehouse 5S, bin numbering, demand forecasting, SKU standard, and ERP requirements brief for November 2026 evaluation. Fee: £2,250/month × 2 months.
  • Workstream 2 — Stock Control App (£4,750 fixed): Real-time stock visibility, bin location tracking, handheld-accessible interface, structured data feed for future ERP. Phase 1 operational by end of Week 4. Phase 2 completion by end of Week 8. Fee: £2,375 (Phase 1) + £2,375 (Phase 2).

4-Week Opt-Out

Both workstreams include a 4-week opt-out review at the end of Week 4:

  • Consultancy: Written notice before end of Week 4 stops Month 2. Month 1 fee is non-refundable.
  • App: Written notice before end of Week 4 stops Phase 2. Phase 1 deposit (£2,375) retained. TackleBag keeps Phase 1 features. No Phase 2 work or IP transferred.

Travel & Expenses

Travel is charged at 45p per mile from Worthing to Twickenham and return, invoiced after each visit. No other expenses are anticipated.
