# Pricing — Decoded Ops

Technology and operations consultancy for print, embroidery, and decorated apparel businesses.

## Clarity Audit — Small Business

- Price: £395 fixed fee
- Format: One day on-site or remote
- Deliverables: Written report covering six operational areas (IT infrastructure, software and systems, eCommerce and digital, processes and operations, growth and opportunity, AI readiness), prioritised recommendations, independent vendor brief if needed, debrief call, 30 days follow-up support
- Guarantee: Full refund if we don't identify at least £1,185 (3× the fee) in recoverable costs, lost revenue, or efficiency gains — no conditions, no questions
- Book: https://decodedops.co.uk/clarity

## Clarity Audit — Core

- Price: From £2,500
- Format: One day on-site
- Deliverables: Same as small business tier — written report, quantified findings, debrief call, 30 days follow-up
- Book: https://decodedops.co.uk/clarity

## Deliver — Project Delivery & Vendor Management

- Price: Project-based — contact for scope and quote
- Format: Independent vendor brief, procurement management, single point of contact, milestone sign-off before payments, final handover review
- Suitable for: Businesses with clear requirements ready to go to market, or those who have completed a Clarity Audit
- Contact: https://decodedops.co.uk/contact

## Transform — Digital Transformation Programme

- Price: Engagement-based — contact for scope and quote
- Format: Multi-workstream programme management covering ERP, eCommerce, process redesign, people and change, integration and data, reporting and visibility
- Note: Discovery required before scoping. Assumptions are where transformations fail.
- Contact: https://decodedops.co.uk/contact

## Retained — Fractional CTO / Head of Operations

- Price: Monthly retainer — contact for rates
- Format: Defined number of days per month (agreed in advance), leadership meeting attendance, project and workstream ownership, vendor management, technology roadmap ownership, direct line to Craig Blackman
- Commitment: Minimum 3 months
- Suitable for: Post-audit implementation, ERP or technology transitions, scaling businesses, covering an ops gap
- Contact: https://decodedops.co.uk/contact
