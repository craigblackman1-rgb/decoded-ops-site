### P2-006: Warm DM Templates ✅
**File:** `P2-006_Warm_DM_Templates.md`

**7 Reusable Templates:**
1. **Consultative Open** (general) — 2-sentence, permission-based
2. **Problem Recognition** (sector-specific) — Speaks to their pain
3. **Post-Engagement Insight Share** — After they engage with your content
4. **Referral Thank You + Offer** — After warm introduction
5. **"I Noticed You..."** (specific observation) — Research-based personalisation
6. **Value-First Share** (no ask) — Article, resource, or insight with no pitch
7. **Direct Ask** (low pressure) — After rapport is established

**Each Template Includes:**
- Base version
- 2–3 contextual variants (by industry, by role, by situation)
- 140+ lines covering response patterns, follow-ups, timing, tracking

**Key Principles:**
- Short (2–3 sentences max for DM)
- Lead with their context, not your expertise
- No sales language ("solution," "reach out," "let me help you grow")
- Sound like a human, not a template
- Make it easy to say yes (20 minutes, no commitment)

**Guidance Included:**
- Response patterns & follow-ups (what to do if no response, if they engage but don't commit, if they say yes)
- Context-specific variations (for ops managers, owners, IT decision-makers)
- Timing & frequency (3–5 per week, spread across 2–3 days)
- Platform notes (LinkedIn DM, email, phone)
- Tracking & iteration (A/B testing recommendations)
