- AI is useful, but only after foundational work

### Sector Observations (Weeks 4, 9)
**Angle:** "What I see regularly..." framing
- Artwork approval bottlenecks
- Order management chaos
- B2B portal misalignment

**Posts should include:**

### Operational Pain (Weeks 5, 10)

- Systems that don't talk (eCommerce + ERP, inventory, invoicing)

**Posts should include:**
- Real costs quantified (15–20 hours/week, one FTE, etc.)
- System disconnects (4–5 different places of truth)
- What visibility looks like

### Client Wins (Weeks 6, 11)

- Approval cycle: 5 days → 24 hours
- ERP replacement: change management that worked

**Posts should include:**
- Challenge (anonymised)

- Results (quantified: time, capacity, accuracy, revenue)
- Pattern: foundational work → system work → results

## Notes & Adjustments

### If Engagement Is Low on a Topic
- Reframe the angle (move from pain to solution)
- Add more specific numbers (quantify the cost)
- Tell a story instead of listing problems
- Shorten the post, make the hook stronger

### If You Get Consistent Feedback
- Create a FAQ post series ("You Asked About X, Here's Y")
- Dig deeper on the topic with follow-up posts
- Create a webinar or longer resource
- Turn into a case study series

### If New Opportunities Emerge
- Replace Week 9–12 posts with timely content
- Pivot based on pipeline (what questions are prospects asking?)
- Create reactive content (industry news + Decoded Ops take)
- Add bonus posts for trending topics
