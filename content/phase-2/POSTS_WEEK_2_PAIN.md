## VARIANT C: Why Most ERP Projects Fail (And How Not To)

The ERP project fails because nobody asked the person using the system whether it actually worked for them. They asked the person who commissioned it. Different question. Different answer.

1. System gets chosen in a meeting. The people who'll use it weren't there.
2. Training happens. It covers how the software works, not how it fits their actual job.
3. By week three, people are reverting to the old way. It's faster.
