## Monthly Rhythm (Weeks 5–16)

- **2–3 posts per week** (Tuesday/Wednesday/Thursday, 8–9 AM UK)
- **Rotates through pillars** to maintain variety and reach different segments of the audience
- **Each post is 150–200 words**, copy-paste ready
- **CTA consistent:** "Free 60-minute discovery call" or "Let's talk"
- **Engagement cadence:** Respond to comments within 2 hours on day of posting

## Success Metrics (Track Weekly)

- Post reach (impressions)
- Engagement rate (reactions + comments + shares)
- Click-through rate (to discovery call booking)
- Lead quality (which posts drive qualified conversations)

---

## Notes

- Vary post length slightly to test LinkedIn's algorithm (150–250 words)
- Mix question-based openers ("What if...?", "Ever noticed...?") with statement-based openers ("Here's what I see...")
- Include 1–2 specific examples per post (real, not hypothetical)
- No hype language. No "game-changer." No buzzwords.
- Every post should be a conversation starter, not a broadcast.
- Respond to comments and build a small community of engaged followers.
